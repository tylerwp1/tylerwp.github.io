What We Do!

So, for all of you wondering, lost internet sheeps who doesn't know who we are or what we do, it's simple. We are Cross Writers, professionals who are interested in improving others' craft of English and writing. So, what do we do? We provide harsh and honest criticism to young writers so that they may better improve their craft. We provide feedback and suggestions to your writing in order to help you develop into the best writer you can be. We specialize in non-fiction writing but writings of any kind are welcomed here. So whether if you are a young writer who is just getting started, a seasoned writer who is already pretty comfortable with your own writing style, or if you are just somebody who wants to publish your piece. You should no doubt submit your writing on our blog. Just know that you better come prepared to get your feelings hurt because we will absolutely criticize the heck out of your piece!

How to use CrossWriters

The process, likewise, is also very simple. Budding or veteran writers can submit their piece on our blog and after a couple days of submission, profession writers here on Cross Writers will give constructed criticism and feedback on your work. This is our process, you guys submit your work onto our webpage, and then after a week, give or so, professional writers here will tell you what could be better, what could be improved, and if you have any questions about our comments. Writers are also available to set up in-person or phone meetings to discuss and explain their feedback. Once you experience the customer service and professionalism that Cross Writers has to offer, you will surely want to join our loyalty program and recommend our service to friends.

About Us

We are Cross Writers! Here in our very neat blog, we are passionate about the success of young, buddling writers! So, in order to do so, what we do is: read, comment, and give constructed criticism to pieces. So, about us here in Cross Writers, we are all professional writers who graduated from college with an English Major. We were inspired to create this website by witnessing the difficulty of managing to get one of our works published. The editing process can sometimes be brutal. Thus, we decided to create a way to simplify the editing process by having it done here, by multiple people at one time.
